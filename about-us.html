Welcome to Bikle Manufacturing

Since the 1940s, Bikle Manufacturing has been a leader in metal cutting machine tools,

we continue our legacy of excellence in manufacturing technology.

Our Mission

To deliver superior metal cutting solutions through precision engineering and innovative manufacturing processes. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality machine tools that enhance their manufacturing capabilities and drive their success.

Our Vision

To be the industry leader in metal cutting technology, setting standards for precision, quality, and innovation. We strive to continuously evolve our capabilities, embrace new technologies, and maintain our position as a trusted partner in manufacturing excellence.
